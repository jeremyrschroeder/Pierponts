ABOUT US

Located in Union Station, Pierpont's is the premier fresh seafood and steak restaurant in Kansas City. The restaurant has an atmosphere of casual elegance and is named after one of the most prominent names in the railroad business, John Pierpont Morgan. Pierpont's has a variety of seafood flown in fresh daily, such as scallops, wild salmon, ahi tuna and lobster. The restaurant also serves, filet mignon, ribeye steak, and Kansas City strip steak. We have an excellent wine cellar that features over 300 bottle selections.

Rich in History & Character

Pierpont's in Union Station, looks much like the original building did in 1914. Sixty percent of the restaurant's three-story structure is historical space, including the original women's waiting room, the children's waiting room and the women's smoking room. Pierpont's was selected as the best architecturally designed restaurant as well as the best places to take out-of-town guests while in Kansas City.

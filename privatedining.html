PRIVATE DINING & CATERING

Pierpont’s can accomodate for events large and small. Our private rooms can accommodate party sizes from 10-80 and provide the perfect setting for your event. Pier Pont’s also provides full-service custom catering. We tailor our services to enhance your ocassion, theme and budget. Our passion for cuisine and exceptional service sets us apart and ensures your event is one to remember.

Wine Cellar Rooms

Pierpont's private dining rooms are located on the lower level, in the wine cellar, and are perfect for your special occasions, business gatherings, or group entertaining. Each room is individually decorated and offers an exceptional place to close the deal, plan a wedding, or have an intimate gathering of friends.

Belvedere Loft

Pierpont’s Belvedere Loft, located on the second floor of Union Station, boasts an exquisite panoramic view of the Grand Hall. With seating for up to 65, and a third floor balcony for cocktail receptions, the Belvedere Loft is the prime destination for upscale private dining, meetings and rehearsal dinners. Enjoy the elegance and reputation of Pierpont's at Union Station in the privacy of the beautiful Belevedere Loft at your next event.

Offsite Catering

Have Pierpont’s at Union Station cater your next event or party. We offer offsite catering with a variety of catering menus to choose from. Or if you need we can create a custom menu for your event. We specializes in wedding receptions and corporate functions. Special dietary requests are no problem.
